Why Technical SEO Matters More Than Ever

In today's competitive digital landscape, technical SEO forms the foundation of any successful search strategy. With Google's algorithm updates increasingly focusing on user experience and page performance, neglecting technical SEO can severely limit your website's potential...

Core Web Vitals: The New Ranking Factor

Google's Core Web Vitals have become critical ranking signals. These metrics measure real-world user experience for loading performance, interactivity, and visual stability:

  • Largest Contentful Paint (LCP): Measures loading performance (should be under 2.5 seconds)
  • First Input Delay (FID): Measures interactivity (should be under 100ms)
  • Cumulative Layout Shift (CLS): Measures visual stability (should be under 0.1)
